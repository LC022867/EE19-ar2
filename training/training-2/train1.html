Anime

Anime is hand-drawn and computer animation originating from
Japan. In Japanese, anime, a term derived from the English
word animation, describes all animated works, regardless
of style or origin. Outside of Japan, anime refers
specifically to animation produced in Japan, or to its common
style, which has since been adopted by a minority of works
produced in other countries.
